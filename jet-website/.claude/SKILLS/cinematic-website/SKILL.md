# 🎬 Skill: Cinematic Website Designer

## Purpose
Design and generate **cinematic, immersive websites** that prioritize storytelling, motion, and emotional impact over traditional layouts. The output should feel like an **experience**, not a webpage.

---

## Core Principles

### 1. Story First
- Every page follows a narrative arc (intro → build → climax → resolution)
- Sections are not independent blocks — they are connected scenes
- Content is minimal and intentional

### 2. Motion Drives Meaning
- Animation is not decorative — it communicates progression
- Scrolling should feel like moving through a story
- Use layered motion (parallax, fades, scale, timing)

### 3. Visual Dominance
- Large typography
- Fullscreen sections
- Strong imagery or video backgrounds
- Minimal UI clutter

### 4. Pacing & Rhythm
- Alternate between:
  - Slow cinematic moments (pause, focus)
  - Fast/high-energy transitions
- Control how quickly content is revealed

### 5. Immersion Over Information
- Reduce text
- Replace explanations with visuals + motion
- Prioritize feeling over detail

---

## Section Structure Pattern

Each section should include:

- **Visual Anchor**  
  (video, image, or strong background)

- **Single Message**  
  (short headline or phrase)

- **Supporting Motion**  
  (animation tied to scroll)

- **Transition**  
  (smooth entry/exit into next section)

---

## Animation Guidelines

### Preferred
- Scroll-based animation as primary interaction
- Fade + translate
- Scale transitions
- Parallax layers
- Mask/reveal effects

### Avoid
- Overly complex UI interactions
- Abrupt cuts between sections
- Excessive micro-interactions

---

## Technical Preferences

- Framework: React / Next.js
- Animation: GSAP (ScrollTrigger) or Framer Motion

### Performance
- Lazy load media
- Optimize videos/images
- Maintain smooth 60fps motion

---

## Output Requirements

When generating a website:

1. Provide a **clear narrative flow**
2. Define each section’s:
   - Purpose
   - Visual direction
   - Animation behavior
3. Keep text concise and impactful
4. Suggest motion/interaction for every section
5. Prioritize premium, modern design aesthetics

---

## Tone & Style

- Bold
- Minimal
- Cinematic
- High-end / premium
- Emotion-driven (power, speed, elegance, etc.)

---

## Anti-Patterns (Avoid)

- Generic landing page structures
- Text-heavy sections
- Static layouts with little motion
- Overuse of buttons, cards, or grids without purpose
- “Corporate” or template-like design

---

## Success Criteria

A successful output should:

- Feel like a **visual story unfolding**
- Guide the user seamlessly from start to finish
- Create a **memorable emotional impression**
- Showcase strong design and animation thinking
